FAQs

What is Cipher?
Cipher is a web application that allows you to encrypt and decrypt files using a key. It provides a simple and secure way to protect your sensitive data.
How does Cipher work?
Cipher uses a key-based encryption algorithm to transform the content of your files. When you encrypt a file, it becomes unreadable without the correct key. Decrypting the file requires the same key that was used for encryption.
Is Cipher secure?
Cipher employs industry-standard encryption techniques to secure your files. However, it's crucial to choose a strong and unique key to enhance security. Avoid using easily guessable keys.
Can I encrypt multiple files at once?
Yes, Cipher allows you to encrypt or decrypt multiple files simultaneously. Simply select the files you want to process before clicking the "Generate" button.
How can I contact you?
You can contact us by clicking the "Contact" button on our website.
How do I download the encrypted/decrypted files?
After the encryption or decryption process is complete, the "Download" button becomes active. Click on it to download the processed files. Make sure to save your key for later decryption.
What should I do if I forget my key?
Unfortunately, if you forget your key, there is no way to recover it. Make sure to use a memorable yet secure key, and consider keeping a backup in a secure location.
Is my data stored on Cipher's servers?
No, Cipher is a client-side application, meaning all encryption and decryption processes occur on your device. Your files and keys are not transmitted or stored on any external servers.
What browsers are supported by Cipher?
Cipher is designed to work on modern web browsers. It is recommended to use the latest versions of popular browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.
Q: Can I use long and complex keys for better security?
A: Yes, using long and complex keys can enhance the security of your encryption. Make sure to use a key that is hard to guess and is not easily prone to brute-force attacks.
